About

Nomad is an e-commerce mobile app based around natural clothing. It’s identity is based around strong feel of community, and fell of nature across full app experience. With a mission to bring more popularity to eco freindly clothing and natural color palettes. There for Nomaad app is based on a friendly approach and accent to community and nature in all it’s features and interface elements.

The Challenge

The main challenge of this app is in its base idea which is creating an immersive experience and feeling of nature's presence, while making sure to maintain a clean modern approach popular among other e-commerce apps, to ensure usability and shopping flow.

The Community

Among other above-mentioned ideas, nomad has a strong idea around the feel of community and people's natural need for connection. Therefore from a warm welcome and call to be part of this very community is the first thing users can see after entering the app.

The Simplicity

Simple, immersive, natural, and clean are few words that may best describe the core values of Nomad's main interface. Based around that feeling of nature's presence shown through highlights and photography, while being clean and immersive for a simple use from exploration all the way to purchase and tracking of the products.

Keep Track

An important part of the community is for sure its connectivity, so we made sure to make it easy to find the closest store at any time. As well as a product tracking system to help you keep track of your delivery process, to never keep you waiting in the house when you can spend than time outside, in nature.
