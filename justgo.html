A new way to experience worldwide traveling

About

Just Go is an all-in-one travel app and its main mission is to make traveling all over the globe easily accessible with just one app.

The Challenge

Designing a new app from the scratch on what’s already hot and quite populated niche is always a challenge by itself. But besides that main challenge of this app lays in the need to design this app around the idea of an all-in-one travel app that makes traveling all over the globe easily accessible and possible with help of just one app. So that people may plan their whole journey from hotels and flights to special trip routes all in one app while also having a full stack of information about the places all over the world.

Strong Identity

Justo Go's identity is based around a mix of high-end look and friendly feeling that builds trust in both the quality of chosen trip or service and its security at the same time.

Aspiring Start

Journey and a great time is the main focus of this app, therefore from the very start, the design is building inspiration for the user. Getting users inspired to travel and feel the moment is one of the main points of conversion in this app.

Joyful Exploration

What makes great places even more inspiring is a joyful exploration of these places. Making sure to create the very best solution and most inspiring exploration experience, I have decided to go with three types of the grid until there is room for further UX research is done on this point. Followed by articles that are neatly related to offered trips, making them even more immersive.

Last Steps

User is ready to travel? With just a few steps he can, not just book the service he desires, he can also get a fully detailed guide of the service, such as a map for the chosen trip. A map covered with all the details about its route as well as details for all the stops and highlights that trip can provide. So no matter if it's a trip, hotel, or flight users will never have to book it based on just a basic list of its features.
