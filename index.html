What I do?

UI DESIGN

No matter if it’s a website, web app, or mobile app each project has a unique look, feel, and general-purpose, so I’ll never jump in with a cookie-cutter solution. Because there is one more thing that ruins a product more than having a bad UI, and that is having refabricated UI.

WEB DEVELOPMENT

In today's day and age, your website is your business card. Based on your project's goals, I design an intuitive, responsive, and fitting website that touches everyone it reaches. The development process is not necessarily code-driven. And tools such as WordPress or Webflow truly make it easier to bring your digital presence to life.

UX RESEARCH

Great visuals don’t mean that much if your customers can’t use your website. And that way collecting user input and identify existing and potential obstacles play a big role in crafting a product that provides seamless interaction and experience.

BRAND IDENTITY / LOGO

While mainly focused on UI, I have a strong passion for building meaningful Brands. Brand identity serves as a key to your business strategy by communicating your functional message, personality, values, and aspirations. Through brainstorm sessions we will discover what it is that makes your brand unique and build the brand from those core values, making sure you stand out from your competition.
