About

Butalq is an e-commerce website that provides timeless peaces for all ages.

The Challenge

Butalq is a unique mix of timeless and modern. A timeless clothing and accessories design combined with a modern identity. Focused on details and geometrical base for flexible composition.

Unique Identity

As mentioned above Butalq is a unique mix of timeless and modern. Therefore its identity lays in the mix of two. Timeless in the simplicity of its color palette and art direction, while modern and artistic with its typography and flexible web composition.

Free Elements

Same as its identity, Butalq's final design is based around mentioned principles. Defined by the geometrical base for its main structure but still flexible and artistic in the freedom of its main elements.
